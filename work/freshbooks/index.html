FreshBooks iOS

Mobile app for FreshBooks

2014-2015

The FreshBooks iOS application was in need of adherence to the iOS 7 Human Interface Guidelines. An improvement to User Experience was also part of this project.

Deference, Clarity, and Depth were the primary iOS 7 principles we followed during this redesign. A complete style guide was created for this project, which allowed for ease of scalability in regards to the visual language of this mobile app.

Assumptions and biases were always put to the test. We sought to challenge iOS 7 conventions, with a goal of understanding the objectives of Apple's HIG. I believe that one must explore divergent paths in interaction and interface principles & aesthetics in order to achieve the final piece of the process.

I like to call this process The Discovery. It allows for one to enter the unknown. The designer creates new roads and close others, falls down hard but picks oneself up again. Process-driven design allows for the designer to get lost in this unknown land, ultimately forging a new road that leads to innovation. We also did some wacky things; it is, after all, part of the creative process.
